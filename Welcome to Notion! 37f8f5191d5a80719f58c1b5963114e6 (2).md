# Welcome to Notion!

- [x]  Create an account with Notion
- [ ]  Tap anywhere and **just start typing**. It’s that simple.
- [ ]  Highlight text and use the menu above your keyboard to **change the style**.
- [ ]  Tap the + icon above your keyboard to **add content**: headings, to-dos, and more.
- [ ]  Collapse your keyboard, then tap and hold this line to **move content**.
- [ ]  Tap the home icon at the bottom left to **see your pages.** (We’ve installed a **To Do List** for you – go check it out!)
- [ ]  Click on the ‣ **Notion AI face** in the bottom right of your screen or [click here](http://notion.so/?target=agent.personal) to see what Agent can do

## 🗺️ **Keep exploring!**

**You’ve just learned the basics.** But there’s so much more to explore — try embedding links and images or creating a new page using the compose icon in the bottom right corner.
