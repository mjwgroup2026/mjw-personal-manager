# GTD System

> **You do not rise to the level of your goals. You fall to the level of your systems.** *– James Clear*
> 
- **How to `?`**
    
    <aside>
    
    - The inbox
    - Process your inbox daily
    - The “Today” tasks view is where your main view to work in
    - Drag and drop tasks into the “Waiting” view if you delegated it or can only proceed after another task has been done
    - Choose your next tasks from the prioritized backlog
    - Tasks of priority “Quick” are usually best done immediately
    - The “Do” date is the day when task is going to be completed
    - The “Due” date is the day *by when* the task should be done
    - Add project-specific views to the “Tasks Overview” section below to only see tasks from that project
    </aside>
